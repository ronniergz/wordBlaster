Word Blaster is a typing game to help you hone your key thrashing skills. You'll have 1 minute to see how many words you can type.

They'll roll in fast and furious from the far reaches of space. Blast them to bits before they hit your ship!

This is a solo project built for the Mintbean JS Hacks: Typing Karaoke hackathon.

The game uses CSS and vanilla javascript to position and animate the key elements. Words are pulled in at random from a separate list file to scroll towards the player.
